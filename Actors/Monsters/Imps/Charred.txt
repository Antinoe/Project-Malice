   Goto See

   Goto See

	TNT1 A 0 A_PlaySound("Charred/Sight")
	CH5R WWWWWWWWWW 3 A_FaceTarget
	CH5R AAAAA 3 A_FaceTarget
	Goto See
  Missile2:
	TNT1 A 0 A_PlaySound("Husk/Charge",CHAN_6)
	CH5R EEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEFFF 1 Light("Avatarfireball4")

  Pain:

    CH5R H 2 A_Pain
    Goto See

    CH5R J 8 A_Scream
    CH5R K 6
    CH5R L 6 A_NoBlocking

    CH5R M -1
    Stop
  XDeath:
	CH5R O 5 A_XScream
	CH5R P 5
	CH5R Q 5 A_NoBlocking
	CH5R RST 5
	CH5R U -1
	Stop
  Raise:
    CH5R ML 8
    CH5R KJI 6
    Goto See
	}
